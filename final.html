About Us

About Ari Feed & Premixes

Founded in 2017, Ari Feed & Premixes has quickly grown into a trusted name in the animal nutrition industry. Our success is built on a foundation of quality, innovation, and responsibility. From the very beginning, we have dedicated ourselves to delivering world-class feed and premix solutions that empower farmers, strengthen livestock health, and support a sustainable food system.

Our Vision

To set global benchmarks in animal nutrition by adopting the best industry practices, integrating advanced technology, and upholding the highest standards of quality and safety.

Our Philosophy

We believe in a quality-first approach, where no compromise is made on raw materials, formulations, or processes. Every product we deliver is backed by rigorous testing, scientific precision, and a deep understanding of the poultry and livestock industry.

What We Do

  • Specialized Feed & Premixes designed for poultry and livestock, ensuring balanced nutrition and enhanced productivity.
  • Research-driven formulations that merge modern nutritional science with practical field expertise.
  • Farmer partnerships, where we act not just as suppliers but as long-term allies in their growth and profitability.
  • Sustainable practices that minimize environmental impact while supporting animal welfare and community well-being.

Our Commitment

At Ari Feed & Premixes, we see our customers as partners in progress. Their trust drives us to innovate, expand, and continuously raise the bar in the feed industry. With a strong focus on quality, customer satisfaction, and sustainable growth, we are shaping a healthier and more productive future for animal farming.
